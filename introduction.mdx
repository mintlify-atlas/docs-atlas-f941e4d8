Automatisch is a self-hosted workflow automation platform that lets you connect services like Slack, GitHub, Gmail, and 90+ other apps to automate your business processes. Unlike cloud-based tools, Automatisch runs on your own infrastructure — so your data never leaves your servers.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Automatisch">
    Deploy with Docker Compose in a single command. See the [installation guide](/guide/installation) for Docker, Render, and production setup options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your apps">
    Go to **My Apps** and add connections to the services you want to automate. Each connection securely stores credentials on your server.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a flow">
    Open **Flows**, create a new flow, add a trigger step, then add one or more action steps. See [Create a flow](/guide/create-flow) for a walkthrough.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and automate">
    Click **Publish** to activate the flow. Automatisch will poll for trigger events and execute your actions automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
